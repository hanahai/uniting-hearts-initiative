GET INVOLVED

We are so happy you’re interested in getting involved with our work here at Uniting Hearts Initiative. There are so many ways for you to help, and we truly appreciate each and every effort. By lending your support, you’ll become a valuable part of our Organization and help to strengthen our operations.

VOLUNTEER YOUR TIME

Sign up to become a volunteer to connect with individuals from nursing homes! This is an easy and efficient way of contributing to the great work we do at Uniting Hearts Initiative. All you have to do is provide your availability and we will organize a online meeting for you. Volunteers will have to apply by filling out the application form and screening through an interview process. Sign up now!

PARTNER WITH US

Our organization always appreciates the generosity and involvement of people like you, with every partnership going towards helping Uniting Hearts connect more residents with volunteers to help combat isolation. Contact us if you are a new nursing home facility or volunteer program interested in working with us!
