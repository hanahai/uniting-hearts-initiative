Cards

We also make cards!

If you are interesting in helping out but you don't have the time to call or video chat, you can contribute by making cards! Contact us down below if you are interested in sending a card to the residents! You do not have to be a registered volunteer to write a card.

On the other hand, if you are a facility that would like us to send cards to your residents, contact us!
